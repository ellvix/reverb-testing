Procedural Reverb Synth

About This Synth

This is a procedural tone generator built with the Web Audio API. It uses a sine wave oscillator as the base tone, and simulates reverb using a custom impulse response buffer (no external audio files required).

  • Oscillator Frequency: sets the pitch (Hz)
  • Tone Duration: how long the tone plays before stopping
  • Reverb Duration: length of the reverb impulse buffer (affects fullness)
  • Reverb Decay: how quickly the reverb fades out
  • Dry/Wet Gain: mix between clean signal and reverb trail

Press Play to generate a tone with your selected settings. Press Stop to immediately kill the sound and reset the context.

Note: Some browsers may require a user gesture (like a click) to start audio.
